Chariz

"The Talkative Queen"

Chariz is a beautiful white Maltese with a calm, confident charm and just the right touch of sass. With her soft, fluffy coat and expressive eyes often peeking through her bangs, she carries herself like she knows she’s the main character of the house.

About Chariz

She has her own way of communicating—when she wants to come inside, she won’t hesitate to bark until someone answers. It’s not just noise, it’s her version of knocking on the door. And when she’s happy (which is often), her whole body joins in—tail wagging, hips swaying, pure excitement in motion.

Despite her playful side, Chariz also loves her comfort. She has a habit of sneaking under wooden chairs for quiet naps, or stretching out belly-up on the floor without a care in the world. It’s her signature “I trust this place completely” pose.

Curious and observant, she enjoys peeking through the gate, watching the outside world like a gentle little guard. And if you ever try howling around her, don’t be surprised—she’ll answer right back, turning it into a full-on conversation.

Chariz isn’t just cute—she’s full of personality, routine, and little quirks that make her unforgettable.
