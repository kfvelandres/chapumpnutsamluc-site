About Chariz

Chariz discovered early on that life is best lived from someone's arms. She has perfected the art of looking irresistibly adorable at the exact moment you need to get something done, making it impossible to put her down. She adores being groomed — the blow dryer, the bows, the brush — she sits still for all of it like a seasoned model at a photo shoot.

Despite her regal demeanor, Mochi is deeply affectionate. She follows her favorite humans from room to room and greets every visitor with a wagging tail and a tilted head. She may be small, but her love fills every corner of the home.
