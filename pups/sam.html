Maltese Pomeranian Shih Tzu Mix

Sam

"The Gentle Gentleman"

Sam is an adorable Maltese–Pomeranian–Shih Tzu mix with a soft coat of light brown fur touched with hints of darker shades, giving him a sweet and expressive look that’s hard to resist. As the son of Pumpkin and Lucas, he carries a mix of playful energy and affectionate charm, making him one of the most lovable companions in the family.

About Sam

Sam is the kind of dog who always wants to be close. Whether you’re sitting on the floor or just relaxing, he’ll come right up and settle beside you—often positioning himself comfortably with his back against you, like he simply belongs there. He’s naturally clingy in the best way, always ready for snuggles and quiet company.

He has a strong love for comfort, especially when it comes to sleep. He prefers the bed, but the moment he feels a little too warm, he’ll move himself to the cooler floor without hesitation. And when it’s time for his afternoon nap, he doesn’t like being alone—Sam will only settle down if someone else is resting too, as if naps are meant to be shared.

Walks are one of his favorite things in the world. When he’s in the mood to go out, he’ll stand up and reach toward you with his front paws, almost like he’s begging you to take him along. And once the walk begins, his excitement shows—he’ll burst into a quick run during the first few minutes, full of energy and joy.

Sam also has a playful, slightly mischievous side—especially when food is involved. If you’re holding something tasty, he’ll repeatedly jump and try to reach your hand, determined and hopeful. And when that doesn’t work, he switches strategies, looking up at you with his signature pitiful eyes, as if he’s silently asking for just one small bite.

Sweet, clingy, and full of personality, Sam has a way of making every moment feel warmer—especially when he’s right there beside you.
