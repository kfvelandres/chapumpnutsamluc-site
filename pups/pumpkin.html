Pumpkin

"The Mealtime Maestro"

Meet Pumpkin, she's a female crossbreed of Maltese and Pomeranian. She's also known as the Pumpkini. She likes to bark when it's almost time for mealtime. She likes to barge the door when she knows that their food is being prepared in the kitchen, she's like a psychic, or maybe she just smells the food.

About Pumpkin

Latte has never met a stranger. He approaches every new person with a slow wag of the tail, a gentle sniff, and the kind of steady eye contact that says "I see you, and I think you're wonderful."
