Maltese Pomeranian Mix

Pumpkin

"The Mealtime Maestro"

Pumpkin is a charming Maltese–Pomeranian mix with a soft blend of light and dark brown fur, giving her a warm, teddy bear-like look that perfectly matches her affectionate personality. She is the daughter of Chariz and the little sister to Peanut, growing up right in the middle of a loving and playful pack. With that, she carries both sweetness and a bit of playful stubbornness, making her impossible to ignore.

PersonalityCharmingMischievousEnergeticResourceful

About Pumpkin

She’s especially expressive when it comes to food—somehow, she always knows when it’s about to be served. The moment she catches the scent, she barks with excitement, as if announcing mealtime to the entire house. And when she wants to be let inside, Pumpkin has her own signature move—jumping and tackling the door with surprising energy, like she’s trying to perform a backflip just to get your attention.

Pumpkin loves the simple joys of life. She enjoys lying on the ground outside, soaking up the warmth of the sun like it’s her personal spa time. But when it comes to affection, she doesn’t wait—she’ll gently reach for your hand and guide it straight to her belly, making it very clear that it’s time for rubs.

She shares a special bond with her mom, Chariz, often enjoying quiet moments where Chariz lovingly cleans her ears. And now, as Sam’s mother, Pumpkin has taken on a caring and gentle role of her own, watching over her little one with patience and warmth.

At night, Pumpkin has her own preferences—she’ll often choose a chair over her pillow, claiming her cozy spot like a little queen settling into her throne. Sweet, playful, and full of personality, Pumpkin brings a unique kind of joy that’s both lively and comforting.
