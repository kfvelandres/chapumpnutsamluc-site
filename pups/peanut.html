Maltese Pomeranian Mix

Peanut

"The Bellyrub Lover"

Peanut is a gentle and observant Maltese–Pomeranian mix with soft, light brown fur that gives her a warm and delicate appearance. As one of Chariz’s daughters and the big sister to Pumpkin, she naturally carries a calm and steady presence, often choosing patience over excitement.

PersonalityPlayfulCalmIndependentSassyPatient

About Peanut

While the others may bark and rush when food is coming, Peanut quietly stands and watches, waiting her turn as if she already understands everything will come to her in time. There’s something quietly confident about her—she doesn’t demand attention, she simply earns it.

The moment you come close, though, Peanut’s affectionate side takes over. She’ll gently lie down and roll onto her back without hesitation, already expecting a belly rub. It’s her way of saying hello—simple, trusting, and full of love.

But she has her playful bursts too. When the gate opens, Peanut wastes no time—she’ll dash outside as fast as she can, turning into a sudden streak of energy that you have to watch out for. It’s a sharp contrast to her usual calm, and it makes her all the more endearing.

She enjoys her quiet spaces as well, often choosing to rest between the vases on the terrace, as if she’s found her own peaceful little corner. And when she wants your attention, she doesn’t bark—she comes close, stands beside you, and gently scratches your leg over and over until you give in to her request for cuddles or belly rubs.

Soft, patient, and deeply affectionate, Peanut has a quiet kind of charm that makes her impossible not to love.

Did you know?

She always assume you will be giving her belly rubs when you come close to her.
