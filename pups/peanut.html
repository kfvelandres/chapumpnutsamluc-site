About Peanut

Life with Boba is never quiet. She announces every delivery truck, every bird, and every leaf that dares to blow past the window. She is the household's unofficial alarm system, head of security, and chief entertainment officer — all at once.

Beneath the bravado, Boba is a deeply playful soul. She lives for fetch, tug-of-war, and any game that ends with her winning. After her many grand adventures (which mostly happen in the backyard), she collapses into a fluffy puddle and sleeps like she just ran a marathon. She is chaotic, she is loud, and she is completely, utterly lovable.
